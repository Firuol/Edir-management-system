Welcome to Ginjo Guduru Edir Management System

Edir is a social institution in Ethiopia used for mutual aid and grants cooperative insurance within specific community. By participating we receive support and encouragement from fellow members, fostering a sense of unity, collaboration and build meaningful connections.
